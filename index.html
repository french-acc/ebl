European Builders League

This is not a regular (boring) hackathon.

There is no theme, no evaluation grid, no winners.

We select a very limited number of participants (25 max). You tell us what you want to build/explore. We give you access to the resources (compute, API credits, mentors...) you need.

You can think of it as a high-signal sandbox where you can explore what you truly care about, while having full access to resources + unlimited RedBull/cool merch.
